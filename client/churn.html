Data Evidence

What We Found in the Data

Three discoveries from 300,000+ customer records that changed how we think about churn.

18.2% of Customers are at Risk Right Now

Our data showed a clear class imbalance — 18% of customers exhibit churn patterns vs 82% retained. This meant standard models would miss 40% of churners by just predicting "stay." We used SMOTE oversampling to fix this.

Purchase Frequency Predicts Churn 3× Better Than Spend

Surprisingly, how often a customer buys matters far more than how much they spend. A customer who buys small items weekly is far more loyal than one who makes large but infrequent purchases.

The First 90 Days Are Make-or-Break

Customers who haven't repurchased within 90 days have a 73% probability of never returning. ROC-AUC of 0.896 confirms the model catches these customers reliably, giving retention teams a clear intervention window.
