Data Evidence

What We Found in the Data

Three demand patterns hidden in 300K+ orders that classical forecasting methods missed.

Demand Has Two Cycles — Weekly AND Monthly

Orders spike every Saturday (+41%) and drop every Monday (−27%). There's also a monthly cycle — first 5 days each month see +23% spend. ML captured both simultaneously; SARIMA handles only one at a time.

Lag Features Predict Future Sales Better Than Trends

14-day and 21-day lagged sales were the most predictive features — beating price, promotions, and seasonality flags. Past behaviour is the best predictor of near-future demand.

LightGBM Beats Statistical Models by 32% on RMSE

SARIMA and Holt-Winters fail on irregular spikes (promotions, weekends). LightGBM learned these non-linear patterns — RMSE: 89 vs 131 for Holt-Winters.
